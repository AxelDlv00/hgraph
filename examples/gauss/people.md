# People & provenance

This is a **per-project blueprint tab** — added through this project's own
`hgraph/config.yaml` under `site.tabs:`, and rendered beside the built-in
Overview / Summary / Graph views. A project can carry any extra content pages it
needs without touching hgraph itself.

## Attribution

- **Statement of the result.** Gauss's summation identity
  $1 + 2 + \cdots + n = \tfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$, attributed to Carl Friedrich Gauss.
- **Formalization.** Synced from the blueprint `.tex` and Lean sources, then
  layered with provenance, comments, and reviews.

Math renders here too: the KaTeX pass runs over custom tabs exactly as it does
over the overview.
